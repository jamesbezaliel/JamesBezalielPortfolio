    About

    Hello, my name is James Bezaliel Jonathan and currently, I am an active student at Bina Nusantara University majoring in Computer Science. I have an interest in working in the field of Information technology, especially in the Front-end field and I have experience using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to work on my college project. I deepen my knowledge and experience of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript when I have free time.     Surely, becoming a professional front-end developer is not easy for me. There are still many weaknesses exist in me. However, I have the persistence to achieve what I aspire to. With hard work and perseverance, I believe that I can achieve what I hope.
